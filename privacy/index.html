Privacy Policy

App: Veloria (Inspiration Dressing) — Package: com.aznablemiao.veloria — Last updated: 2025-12-10

Welcome to Veloria. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Information you provide directly: such as profile details, feedback, and any content (text, photos) you upload.
  • Device and usage information: technical data about your device and app usage (e.g., device model, OS version, crash logs, usage metrics) to improve app performance.
  • Data related to permissions: if you enable certain features, we may access related data (for example, photos or camera images). See the Permissions section below.

2. Permissions & How We Use Them

The app may request the following sensitive permissions only when you enable the related feature:

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA) — used to take photos for outfit reference or style capture. Requested only when you choose the camera option.
  • Storage (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) — used to read or save images and cache files when required by your device/Android version.
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) — used only if you enable voice input or recording features.
  • Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) — used optionally for location-based recommendations if you opt in.

Important: Permissions are requested on an opt-in basis. If you deny a permission, the related feature will not work but the app's core functionality remains available.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app and its features (e.g., personalized recommendations).
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues and monitor app performance.
  • To communicate with you about updates, support requests, or important notices.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or other illegal activity.

  • Cloud hosting or storage providers used to store your content when you choose to back up or sync.

We require third parties to process data only as instructed and to implement reasonable security measures. If you use features that integrate with specific third-party SDKs (e.g., ads or social logins), we recommend listing those SDKs and linking to their privacy policies on this page.

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or to comply with legal obligations. When you request deletion of your data, we will delete or anonymize personal data within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required by law.
